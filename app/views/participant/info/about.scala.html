Data handling and confidentiality

Your data will be processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation 2016 (GDPR). Although we will be asking for your email in order to send you questionnaires and information, your data will not be linked to your name and you will remain fully anonymous throughout. Data will be stored on Qualtrics (questionnaire website). Your data will be deleted within 7 years of the study termination. Your data will only be shared with the research team.

If you would like more information about how your data will be processed in accordance with GDPR please visit this page.
